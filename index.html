Biography

I am a first year Ph.D. student at MMLab, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Tianfan Xue. My research interests lie in the fields of computer vision and deep learning, and I am currently focusing on controllable video generation.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
